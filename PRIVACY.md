# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 8, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use the `cssvg-icons` library.

## 1. Data Collection

The `cssvg-icons` library is a client-side icon system. We **do not collect, store, or share** any personal data or usage information from developers or end-users.

## 2. Third-Party Services

The library itself does not interact with any third-party services that track users. However, if you use the library within a framework (like Next.js) or host it on a platform (like Vercel or GitHub), those platforms may have their own privacy policies regarding traffic and usage.

## 3. Local Storage

The library does not use cookies or local storage to track users.

## 4. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## 5. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the maintainers via [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/Harijohnson/cssvg-icon/issues).
